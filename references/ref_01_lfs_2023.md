# Reference [1]: Sri Lanka Labour Force Survey (LFS) 2023

### **Key Data Points for 2023**
*   **Total Employed Population:** 8.01 Million (down from 8.15M in 2022).
*   **Unemployment Rate:** 4.7% (remained stable but youth unemployment is higher at 17%).
*   **Labour Force Participation:** 48.6% (lowest in recent years).

### **Sector Breakdown**
*   **Services:** 48.4% (The largest employer and contributor to GDP).
*   **Agriculture:** 26.5% (Employs over a quarter of the population but contributes only 8.3% to GDP).
*   **Industry:** 25.1%.
