# Reference [13]: Design Science Research Guidelines (Hevner et al., 2004)

### **The 7 Guidelines for DSR**
This is a critical reference for your **Methodology (Phase 7)**.

1.  **Design as an Artifact:** Creating a viable, purposeful artifact (in your case, the NexusHR AI module).
2.  **Problem Relevance:** Addressing an important and relevant business problem (SME employee attrition).
3.  **Design Evaluation:** Rigorously evaluating the artifact's utility, quality, and efficacy (your Recall, Cost, and SUS metrics).
4.  **Research Contributions:** Providing clear and verifiable contributions (the cost-efficiency findings).
5.  **Research Rigor:** Applying rigorous construction and evaluation methods.
6.  **Design as a Search Process:** Treating design as an iterative search for an effective solution.
7.  **Communication of Research:** Effectively communicating the findings to both technical and management audiences.
