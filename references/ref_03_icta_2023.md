# Reference [3]: SL Digital Economy Report 2023 (ICTA)

### **Key Metrics for 2023**
*   **GDP Contribution:** Digital economy contributes ~4.4% to national GDP.
*   **2030 Target:** Aiming for 12% GDP contribution and USD 15 Billion value.
*   **Digital Literacy:** 64% (General literacy), but Computer Literacy is lower at **40%**.
*   **SME Adoption:** Only 18.6% of Sri Lankan firms have a website.
