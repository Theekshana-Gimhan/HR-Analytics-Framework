# Reference [17]: Brain Drain Data (SLBFE, 2023)

### **Key Data Points for 2023**
*   **Total Departures:** 294,314 (registered for foreign employment).
*   **Professional Migration:** Over 17,000 professionals left in 2023.
*   **Intent to Migrate:** Nearly 900,000 passports issued in the peak of the crisis.
