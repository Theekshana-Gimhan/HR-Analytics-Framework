# Reference [12]: The "Data Silo" Problem (Angrave et al., 2016)

### **Core Problem**
HR analytics often fails because HR data is isolated in "silos," disconnected from financial and operational metrics. This makes it impossible to show how "People" affect "Profit."

### **The Solution**
Integrating HR data with other datasets to move beyond simple "headcount" reporting and toward **Predictive Analytics**.

### **Use in Viva**
"Angrave (2016) warns that HR analytics fails when data is siloed. My project solves this by **architectural integration**—embedding the AI module directly into the *NexusHR* operational system, so it can 'see' attendance, leave, and payroll data in one place."
