# Reference [7]: National AI Strategy (Ministry of Tech, 2024)

### **Core Vision**
To create a "Digitally Empowered Sri Lanka" by 2028 and increase the digital economy's share of GDP to 15% by 2030.

### **Key Pillars Relevant to You**
*   **Quick Wins:** The strategy prioritizes pilot projects that show immediate value.
*   **Democratization:** Making AI accessible beyond large corporations.
*   **Priority Sectors:** Finance, Health, and **Public Services/Governance** (which includes HR Management).

### **Use in Viva**
"My project directly supports the National AI Strategy's goal of 'Rapid Value Realization.' By building an affordable attrition predictor, I am providing an 'AI Quick Win' for the Sri Lankan SME sector."
