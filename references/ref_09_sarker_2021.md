# Reference [9]: Machine Learning Algorithms (Sarker, 2021)

### **Core Findings**
In his 2021 study, I. H. Sarker explores employee attrition prediction using various machine learning algorithms, including **Logistic Regression, Decision Trees, Random Forests, Support Vector Machines (SVM), and K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN)**.

### **Key Insights for Viva**
*   **Best Performers:** **Random Forest** and **Decision Tree** models typically outperform others in terms of accuracy and predictive power for identifying at-risk employees.
*   **Feature Engineering:** The research emphasizes that specific factors like job satisfaction, work-life balance, and monthly income are crucial for improving model performance.
*   **Strategic Value:** The study concludes that machine learning provides a proactive, data-driven framework for HR departments to mitigate turnover and enhance organizational stability.
