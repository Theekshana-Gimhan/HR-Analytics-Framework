# Reference [2]: Digital Transformation in Sri Lankan SMEs (Fernando & Perera, 2023)

### **Core Findings**
SMEs contribute over 52% of Sri Lanka's GDP but face significant structural and cultural barriers to adopting digital technologies.

### **Key Challenges Identified**
1.  **Capital:** High initial costs for software and infrastructure.
2.  **Skills Gap:** Critical shortage of technical staff within small firms.
3.  **Security Fears:** Hesitation to move data to the cloud due to perceived privacy risks.
4.  **Economic Volatility:** Recent crises have forced SMEs to prioritize survival over long-term tech investments.

### **Strategic Insight**
Successful digital adoption in Sri Lanka is driven by **simplicity in user experience**. This justifies your focus on a simple "Insights Dashboard" for non-technical managers.
