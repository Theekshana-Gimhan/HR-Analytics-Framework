# Reference [11]: MLaaS Architecture (Ribeiro et al., 2015)

### **Core Architecture**
Ribeiro et al. (2015) propose a **Machine Learning as a Service (MLaaS)** architecture that leverages cloud computing to provide automated machine learning workflows through accessible APIs and interfaces.

### **Key Benefits for SMEs**
*   **Abstraction:** It abstracts complex infrastructure and algorithmic details, allowing SMEs to integrate predictive analytics without specialized hardware or deep data science expertise.
*   **Cost Reduction:** Reduced operational costs and faster time-to-market for ML features.
*   **Scalability:** Ability to scale resources dynamically based on business needs.
*   **Democratization:** Enables smaller organizations to compete with larger enterprises in data-driven decision-making.
