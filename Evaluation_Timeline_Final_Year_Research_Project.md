# Final Year Individual Research Project (COM4901) - Official Project Evaluation Timeline

## 1. Introduction
This document outlines the official evaluation timeline for the Final Year Individual Research Project (COM4901). The timeline is designed to guide students, internal supervisors, and external supervisors through all required milestones and assessments.

**Important Supervisor Review Requirement:**  
Before any formal submission to the LMS, students **must share the completed work with their respective supervisor(s) at least one (01) week in advance** for review, corrections, and approval. Only supervisor-approved documents are permitted for LMS submission.

## 2. Project Timeline and Milestones

### Phase 1: Proposal Development and Submission
* Research problem refinement and scope finalization with supervisors
* Literature review and methodology development
* **Proposal document submission – 03 March 2026**
* Proposal evaluation and feedback
* **Assessment Component:** Proposal Writing (15%)

### Phase 2: Proposal Presentation
* Preparation of proposal presentation
* **Proposal presentation and evaluation – One week after the proposal report submission (02–08 March 2026)**
* Approval of proceeding to full implementation
* **Assessment Component:** Proposal Presentation (10%)

### Phase 3: Research Implementation and Interim Evaluation
* System design, model formulation, or experimental setup
* Development, implementation, and experimentation
* Preliminary results analysis
* **Interim report submission – 29 June 2026**
* Interim evaluation and structured feedback
* **Assessment Component:** Interim Report (15%)

### Phase 4: Final Development and Report Submission
* Final system enhancement and optimization
* Complete results analysis and discussion
* Final report writing and formatting
* **Final report submission – 31 August 2026**
* Plagiarism checking and compliance verification
* **Assessment Component:** Final Report (30%)

### Phase 5: Final Presentation and Project Demonstration
* Preparation for final presentation and demonstration
* **Final presentation and project demonstration – One week after the final report submission (02–09 September 2026)**
* Final marking moderation and result finalization
* **Assessment Component:** Presentation and Demonstration (30%)

## 3. Assessment Weightage Summary

| Assessment Component | Weightage (%) |
| :--- | :--- |
| Proposal Writing | 15 |
| Proposal Presentation | 10 |
| Interim Report | 15 |
| Final Report | 30 |
| Presentation and Demonstration | 30 |
| **Total** | **100** |

## 4. Conclusion
All students are expected to strictly adhere to this timeline. Any deviations must be formally communicated and approved through the respective supervisors and the faculty. Completion of all assessments and evaluations will be finalized in accordance with the timeline specified in this document and relevant academic regulations.
